In Your Timezone

Distribute times easily
Current Timezone is a simple tool you can use to distribute time information in such a way that it will automatically be localized to the user's timezone. To use it just send the link https://time.cs61a.org/t?1593730500 where you can replace the part after the t? with the UNIX timestamp of the time you want to distribute.
